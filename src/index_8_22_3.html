Research Results: MSc Thesis

In my MSc thesis, I analyzed and compared four different models for multi-label classification: LIFT, Deep Learning (RBMs), and Graph-based models. I focused on evaluating these models using metrics like Accuracy, Hamming Loss, and Average Precision.

Here are the key results:

Algorithm Genbase (Accuracy) Yeast (Accuracy) Enron (Accuracy) Image (Accuracy)
LIFT 0.982 0.5194 0.3497 0.4356
Three-layered RBM 0.0089 0.4566 0.3072 0.3653
Propagation with extra nodes 0.9933 0.541 0.3269 0.4403

Overall, the LIFT technique showed the best results for the Average Precision metric across all datasets. However, the Propagation with extra nodes approach outperformed in Accuracy for all datasets except Enron and showed better Hamming Loss for Genbase and Enron datasets.

Technologies Used: Python, Matplotlib, Matlab, Scikit-learn
