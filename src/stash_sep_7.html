MSc Thesis: Multilabel Classification

I analyzed and compared four different models for multi-label classification: LIFT, RBMs, and Graph-based models. The analysis was based on Accuracy, Hamming Loss, and Average Precision metrics. Below are the results of the different models tested on various datasets:

Algorithm Genbase (Accuracy) Yeast (Accuracy) Enron (Accuracy) Image (Accuracy)
LIFT 0.982 0.5194 0.3497 0.4356
Three-layered RBM 0.0089 0.4566 0.3072 0.3653
Propagation with extra nodes 0.9933 0.541 0.3269 0.4403

The LIFT technique had the best results for the Average Precision metric, while the Propagation with extra nodes approach showed better Accuracy and Hamming Loss for most datasets.

Technologies Used: Python, Matplotlib, Matlab, Scikit-learn
